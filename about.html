The Show

About Fake & Gabe

Everyone's performing. Curated feeds, rehearsed answers, the version of yourself you post but don't live. Fake & Gabe is the conversation that happens after the cameras would normally turn off — if there were any cameras.

Each episode is unscripted: real opinions, real arguments, and the occasional regret the next morning. No talking points, no brand safety, just two perspectives working through whatever's actually going on.

What to Expect

Weekly episodes covering culture, current events, relationships, and whatever's been argued about in the group chat that week. Long-form, occasionally chaotic, always honest.

The Name

Half the world is performing for an audience. We're the other half — calling it out, laughing about it, and admitting when we're guilty of it too.
