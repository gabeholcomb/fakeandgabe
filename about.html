The Show

About Fake & Gabe

We live in a world that's constantly performing. Curated takes, political spin, cultural pressure to go along with whatever the loudest voices are saying. Fake & Gabe exists to cut through that — and to do it honestly, from a Christian perspective.

Gabe tackles the topics people are actually debating — culture, politics, religion, current events — and brings a Biblical worldview to the conversation. Not lecturing. Not pretending to have all the answers. Just committed to speaking truth in a world that often prefers comfortable lies.

Why "Fake"?

Pastor Andrew Isker's book The Boniface Option calls our current culture "Trashworld" — a system engineered to strip life of meaning, isolate people from each other, and keep them docile. His prescription? Don't retreat. Confront it. Name it for what it is. Present Christ confidently and without apology.

That's the spirit behind Fake & Gabe. We're not tiptoeing around what's broken in the culture. We're naming it, examining it, and pointing back to the Truth — the same way St. Boniface didn't negotiate with the oak. He cut it down.

What You'll Hear

Weekly conversations on culture, politics, faith, and the stories shaping our world. Real opinions, grounded in Scripture, delivered straight. No talking points — just Gabe being honest about where he stands and why.
